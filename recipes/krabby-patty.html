Krabby Patty

Description

The Krabby Patty is the most delectable item on out list brought to you by our favorite sponge

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger seasoning (if desired)
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced cheese (Bikini Bottom or Cheddar)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Sliced pickle
  • Lettuce

Steps:

To make a krabby patty follow these steps secretly

  1. Organize the ground beef into a long layer. Make to desired burger thickness.
  2. Cut round, patty shapes for the Krabby patties.
  3. Lay the Krabby patties on a grill or frying pan. Heat 500ÂºF for 5-10 minutes on each side for a nice medium to well done patty.
  4. Lay the cheese on each of the patties and let melt for 2 minutes.
  5. Take the patties off the grill. Lay them onto the buns.
  6. Add the condiments as listed in the ingredients above.
  7. For the bonus secret mix ketchup and mayonnaise with a very small drop of hot sauce and add to the Krabby patties
