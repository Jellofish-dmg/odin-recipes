Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a french dish which is typically a stew it combines quite a bit vegetables we'll discuss further below.

Ratatouille has been made popular largely due to the pixar movie about a rat who can cook and makes the dish in the finale of the movie.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Courgette
  • Aubergine
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • Marjoram
  • Fennel and basil
