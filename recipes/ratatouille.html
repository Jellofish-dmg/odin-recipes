Ratatouille

Description

Ratatouille is a french dish which is typically a stew it combines quite a bit vegetables we'll discuss further below.

Ratatouille has been made popular largely due to the pixar movie about a rat who can cook and makes the dish in the finale of the movie.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Courgette
  • Aubergine
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • Marjoram
  • Fennel and basil

Steps

To make ratatouille follow these steps:

  1. In a skillet, cook the garlic in olive oil. Add the eggplant, parsley, and salt.
  2. Cook until the eggplant is tender, then spread the mixture in a prepared casserole dish and sprinkle with Parmesan.
  3. Spread the zucchini on top and sprinkle with more cheese. Continue layering according to the instructions in Step 3.
  4. Bake the ratatouille in the preheated oven until the vegetables are tender.
