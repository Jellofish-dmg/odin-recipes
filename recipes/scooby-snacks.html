Scooby Snacks

Description:

These snacks are like nom other a taste that will surely leave you speechless

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 2 cups oats
  • 1/2 cup honey

Steps:

To make scooby snacks follow these steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the peanut butter, oats, and honey.
  2. Stir well until all the ingredients are fully incorporated.
  3. Shape the mixture into small bite-sized bars or balls.
  4. Place the Scooby Snacks on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Refrigerate the snacks for at least 1 hour to firm up.
  6. Enjoy! Serve the delicious Scooby Snacks and indulge in their irresistible flavor.
